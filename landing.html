Safety Tips for Meeting Strangers

Follow these guidelines to maximize your safety

üè† Never Meet at Your Home

Protect your privacy and personal safety by avoiding home meetings. Always choose public locations.

üë• Bring a Friend

If possible, take a trusted friend or family member with you for added security and peace of mind.

üöî Meet at Safe Locations

Police or fire stations are the safest options. Otherwise, choose busy public places with security cameras.

üí° Well-Lit Public Spaces

Choose busy, well-monitored areas like shopping centers, restaurants, or coffee shops during business hours.

üíµ Avoid Carrying Cash

Use credit/debit cards or digital payment methods to minimize theft risk and avoid unnecessary vulnerability.

üì± Keep Phone Charged

Ensure your phone is fully charged before meetings. Consider bringing a portable charger for extended meetings.

About SafeMeetAlert

SafeMeetAlert was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves to feel safe when meeting new people. Whether you're meeting someone from an online marketplace, going on a first date, or conducting business with a stranger, personal safety should never be compromised.

In today's connected world, we meet more strangers than ever before. From online dating to e-commerce transactions, ride-sharing to freelance work‚Äîmodern life requires us to trust people we've never met. While most interactions are perfectly safe, having a safety net provides invaluable peace of mind.

Our Mission

To empower people to meet confidently by providing intelligent, non-intrusive safety monitoring that activates only when needed. We believe safety technology should be invisible when everything is fine, but instantly accessible when it's not.

Why SafeMeetAlert?

Smart Monitoring: Unlike constant tracking apps, SafeMeetAlert only monitors during scheduled meetings. Your privacy is protected, and your battery life is preserved.

Family & Friends as Guardians: Instead of relying on strangers or call centers, SafeMeetAlert empowers your trusted network. Your emergency contacts receive real-time updates and can take immediate action if needed.

Grace Period Intelligence: We understand meetings run long. Our 10-minute grace period prevents false alarms while ensuring genuine emergencies trigger immediate alerts.

Simple by Design: Safety tools should be easy to use under stress. SafeMeetAlert's one-tap emergency button and automatic notifications mean help is always just seconds away.

Our Commitment

We are committed to protecting your data, respecting your privacy, and continuously improving our safety features. SafeMeetAlert never sells your data, never shares your location without permission, and never compromises on security.

Who Uses SafeMeetAlert?

Online Sellers & Buyers: Meet safely when buying or selling items from online marketplaces like Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, or OfferUp.

First Dates: Enjoy dating apps with an extra layer of security. Let trusted friends monitor your first meetings.

Freelancers & Contractors: Meet new clients confidently, especially for home services or in-person consultations.

Parents & Students: College students, young professionals, and parents can all benefit from having a safety companion.

Anyone Meeting Strangers: From rideshare meetups to real estate viewings, SafeMeetAlert adapts to any situation where you're meeting someone new.

Join Our Community

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

SafeMeetAlert collects the following information to provide our safety monitoring service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Emergency Contacts: Names, phone numbers, and email addresses of contacts you add
  • Meeting Details: Information about scheduled meetings including location, time, and person you're meeting
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you enable location tracking during meetings
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, and technical diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide and improve SafeMeetAlert services:

  • Send SMS and email alerts to your emergency contacts during meetings
  • Track your location in real-time when you enable GPS tracking
  • Send emergency alerts if you don't check in after the grace period
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure account security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Emergency Contacts: Meeting details and location data are shared with contacts you explicitly designate
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect safety
  • Service Providers: Third-party services (SMS, email, maps) that help us operate SafeMeetAlert, under strict confidentiality agreements

4. Location Data Privacy

Location tracking is entirely under your control:

  • GPS tracking only activates when you enable it for specific meetings
  • Location data is only collected during active meetings
  • You can disable location tracking at any time
  • Location history is retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
  • Emergency contacts can only see your location when you've scheduled a meeting with tracking enabled

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for location data transmission
  • Secure password hashing (passwords are never stored in plain text)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure servers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Two-factor authentication available for all accounts

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information anytime
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your meeting history and contact information
  • Opt-Out: Disable SMS notifications or email alerts at any time

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 2 years are automatically deleted
  • Meeting History: Retained for 1 year, then archived or deleted
  • Location Data: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

8. Children's Privacy

SafeMeetAlert is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Parents may create accounts for their children and manage them as guardians.

9. International Users

SafeMeetAlert operates globally. By using our service, you consent to data transfer to the United States where our servers are located. We comply with GDPR for EU users and applicable privacy laws worldwide.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email. Continued use of SafeMeetAlert after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
